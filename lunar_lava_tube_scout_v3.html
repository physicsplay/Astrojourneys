🎯 Learning target

Master ONE relationship — echo ranging:
d = v·t/2 (depth = signal speed × round-trip time ÷ 2)
Forward, inverse, and under a cave-in clock.

⚠️ Misconception to break

"The echo time IS the depth." No — the pulse travels down AND back. The depth is HALF of what the round trip suggests. The ÷2 is the whole game.

🧪 Model & assumptions (stated honestly)

Signal speed in a medium is v = c/√εᵣ; here v is given per material and the graded law is d = v·t/2 (permittivity εᵣ is the "why", not graded) · working units: d in metres, v in ×10⁸ m/s, t in microseconds (µs), so d = 50·ṽ·t · one flat reflector per ping, normal incidence · the real GPR trade-off (frequency ↔ penetration ↔ resolution) is acknowledged in this briefing but never graded · teaching εᵣ rounded to clean (c/v)².

Difficulty generous tolerances

📐 Key formulas (given)

echo ranging: d = v · t / 2 working form: d[m] = 50 · ṽ · t ṽ = v / 10⁸ (m/s) · t in µs inverse gate: t = 2d / v medium speed: v = 2d / t (then v = c/√εᵣ)
