PHYSICS

Normal force
N = m g cosθ
Traction condition
Uphill: μ ≥ tanθ + crr
Downhill: μ ≥ tan|θ| − crr
Resistive force
↑ F = m g (sinθ + crr cosθ)
↓ F = m g (crr cosθ − sinθ) if > 0
Work
W = F s
Power
P = F v
Time
t = s / v
Why g cancels in the slip condition:
Uphill: μ · mgcosθ ≥ mgsinθ + c_rr · mgcosθ
→ μ ≥ tanθ + c_rr
Downhill: μ · mgcos|θ| ≥ mgsin|θ| − c_rr · mgcos|θ|
→ μ ≥ tan|θ| − c_rr   (steep downhill can still cause slips!)
g gone in both cases! Mars or Earth — same traction physics.
