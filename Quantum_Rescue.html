🎯 Learning target

Master ONE relationship — the photoelectric equation:
Kmax = hf − W = 1240/λ[nm] − W [eV]
Used by hand in every stage: forward, inverse, and to identify an unknown metal.

⚠️ Misconception to break

"Brighter light gives electrons more energy." No: intensity only changes HOW MANY photons arrive. Energy is paid per photon, E = hf. Below the work function, a floodlight achieves nothing.

🧪 Model & assumptions (stated honestly)

Clean metal surfaces in vacuum · teaching value hc ≈ 1240 eV·nm (exact: 1239.84) · work functions are standard teaching values · stopping potential Vs[V] equals Kmax[eV] numerically · electron flight animation slowed ~10⁹× (real transit: nanoseconds) — the physics itself is never altered.
